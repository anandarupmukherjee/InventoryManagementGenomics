Help & User Guide

This guide covers key workflows in the Inventory Management System, where to find them, and tips to avoid common issues.

Navigation

  • Dashboard (Navbar > Dashboard): System status, charts, quick links. Help link lives here.
  • Product List (Navbar): Browse/search products; scan a barcode to filter.
  • Stock Management (Navbar > Stock Management):
    • Stock Admin: Add/edit products and lots.
    • Record/Track PO: Purchase order capture and tracking.
    • Allocation (Location): Location tracking overview, add stock to location, transfer stock.
  • Reports & Analytics (Navbar):
    • Track Withdrawals/Low/Expired: Operational reports.
    • Download Report: CSV/Excel exports.
    • Forecasting and Intelligence (Experimental): Advanced insights.
  • Management (Navbar): Users, suppliers, locations, product codes, password.

Core Tasks

  • Register Stock (Stock Management > Register Stock): Scan a barcode to increment a lot; if the lot does not exist, it is created automatically.
  • Create Withdrawal (Navbar): Barcode-first; manual mode dropdown only shows items with stock. Supports partial and volume withdrawals.
  • Stock Admin (Stock Management > Stock Admin): Add/edit products and lots; supplier ref is the authoritative supplier value (auto-prefills on scanned products).
  • Location Allocation (Stock Management > Allocation (Location)):
    • Add Stock to Location: Shows “Current Allocation for this Lot” table so you know per-location quantities before adding.
    • Transfer Stock: Also shows “Current Allocation for this Lot”; only allows source locations with stock to reduce errors.
    • Overview: Filterable table of location stocks.
  • Quality Control (Navbar when enabled): Record QC checks. Product Lots QC shows in-stock lots only with color status (Green=Pass, Red=Fail, Amber=Pending/Waiting).

Analytics & Intelligence

  • Download Report (Reports & Analytics): Export a single selected model with date filters; filenames are IMS_<user>_<date>.
  • Forecasting (Reports & Analytics): Demand/stock insights (experimental).
  • Intelligence (Experimental) (Reports & Analytics): Charts and tables for suggested location transfers, expiry/ageing buckets, and slow movers.

Tips

  • Use barcode scanning where possible; fields auto-populate product, lot, expiry, and supplier ref.
  • For location moves, use “Current Allocation for this Lot” tables in Add/Transfer Stock (Stock Management > Allocation) to pick valid source locations and quantities.
  • QC lots from Product Lots QC; color codes: Green = Pass, Red = Fail, Amber = Pending/Waiting.
  • Review Intelligence for suggested transfers and at-risk lots to reduce stockouts and expiry waste.
